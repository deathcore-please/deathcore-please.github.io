About

I am an avid FPV hobbyist, and discovered FPV back in 2019 during the first year of my undergraduate degree. Starting off with being awed by my neighbour who built RC fixed-wing models, I started experimenting with ardupilot and pixhawk based drones under his guidance before moving on to FPV. And since then, I have never looked back.

With experience ranging from shooting commercially, to building custom quads for various clients, this hobby has a special place in my heart which taps onto my dreams of becoming a fighter pilot (its still a dream btw) as well irks my inner engineer. On that note,if you like my work, feel free to hit me up for any FPV shoots in London/Delhi (depending on where I currently am)!

Freestyle

Although I pursue this hobby purely for fun, I pride myself as a freestyle pilot from India. We have a huge and passionate FPV community, but lack international representation and development of freestyle skills. I want to change that and prove that freestyle talent in India exists (...my friend says there are more direct ways to sing praises for yourself). Here are a few freestyle videos I could salvage. A lot more of them exist which I either couldn't dig up or aren't good enough. And yeah, more are definitely on the way!
