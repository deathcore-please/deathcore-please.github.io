About

Hello fellow musicians and peasants! Welcome to this pitstop where you can know everything about my services as a musician! I have been an avid musician for more than 18 years and have been playing in professional gigs for more than 7 years. Music being my strongest passion among all my hobbies, I believe I have spent the most time not working as an engineer behind the drumkit.

My expertise lies in various metal subgenres, especially deathcore, metalcore, groove metal, tech-death, etc., although I have worked in various other non-metal gigs as well (especially Bollywood and Western Rock). I am a drummer, distortion vocalist, and music producer, with secondary skills in various other instruments. Please contact me via my email or instagram below for any business/work enquiries (or if you just wanna jam)! If you're here just for fun, then why not check out my YouTube Music playlist down below. Pretty solid bangers in there I promise.
